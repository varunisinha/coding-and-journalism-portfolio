About Me

I am a New York City-based writer, photographer, and multimedia producer. I am trained as a cultural journalist with expertise in literature and visual studies. I have written reviews for The Post and Courier, quirky pieces for Time Out New York, and produced Wikipedia entries on art for the Guggenheim. I started my career as a copywriter, creating 360-degree campaigns for global brands such as Panasonic, Yamaha, Alstom, and the Government of India. I am proficient in writing copy for print advertisements, scripting television commercials, feature films, and radio jingles. I have experience curating click worthy digital content for social media across channels aimed at diverse audiences.

I have skills in front-end programming with working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I can work with clients to develop the overall look and design of a website, produce and maintain user interfaces, and ensure their designs are responsive to platforms such as laptops and mobile phones. I can create tools that enhance the user's website experience and incorporate applications, graphics, audio, and video clips. I can also routinely test the websites for ease of use, speed, and fix any bugs that arise.
